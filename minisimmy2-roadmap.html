v1.9+
Music Reactivity System
• Upload & select songs from a dedicated folder
• Real-time audio analysis (frequency bands, beat detection, energy)
• Multiple reactive parameters controlled by different parts of the music:
– Vortex strength & fall speed
– Food node size pulsing (green/blue)
– Red hazard / cursor hazard spawn frequency
– Energy wall pulsing and movement
– Creature movement jitter / responsiveness
– Screen shake or particle intensity on bass
• Sliders to map frequency bands to different game systems
• Presets for different music genres / reactivity styles
• Toggleable music player window with visualizer
Detailed implementation plan to be provided by user

v3.0+ (Major Long-term Feature)
Paragon / Prestige System – Lineage Legacy & Neural Persistence
Core Fantasy: Creatures can achieve transcendence through the vortex. Instead of being forgotten, worthy individuals return as enhanced Paragons carrying condensed wisdom from their previous life, enabling true long-term individual progression and bloodline legacy.
Paragon Emergence
• Extend module tiers from V → X (Tier X is significantly harder to reach)
• Tier X creatures have a chance to become Paragon Candidates
• Vortex can rarely "call" a worthy creature to become a Paragon
• Paragons are visually distinct (strong glow, unique particles, subtle halo/aura)
Prestige / Rebirth Mechanics
• Trigger costs 100 million High Roller Points
• Full map reset occurs upon Paragon activation (dramatic moment)
• Unspent High Roller Points can be converted into a new persistent currency: Aetherium Crystals
• Player chooses one eligible creature to Paragon
• Player then selects a powerful lineage gene/upgrade for that bloodline
• Future newborns have a chance to spawn carrying that Paragon gene
Neural / Memory Preservation (High Priority)
• Preserve as much "brain" information as possible in the newborn Paragon
• Carry over the creature’s traits object (hazard avoidance, exploration bias, vortex resistance, pheromone sensitivity)
• Retain specialization bias and some behavioral memory
• First prestige: 1 special Paragon Module (unique, powerful module normal creatures cannot obtain)
• Subsequent prestiges in the same lineage: additional special nodes/modules can be gained
• Long-term goal: Player can select which special module the Paragon receives upon prestige
Meta Progression (Aetherium Crystals)
• New long-term currency earned by converting excess High Roller Points
• Dedicated Meta Upgrade Menu paid with Aetherium Crystals
• Upgrades include: Increased Paragon spawn frequency, stronger starting bonuses for Paragons, more powerful lineage genes, etc.
• Creates a true roguelike/meta layer on top of the core simulation
Lineage & Bloodline Feel
• Paragons and their descendants feel like distinct, persistent individuals across resets
• Strong incentive for long-term play and "cultivating legendary bloodlines"
• Potential for visible lineage tracker or Paragon history in future UI
High-priority long-term vision. Should be built in clear phases so it doesn’t destabilize the core simulation.
