Future Development Phases

v1.7 (Next)
Energy Net – Advanced Filters & Physics
• Improved scooping physics (creatures bunch realistically when dragging fast)
• Much more granular filter options (specific module counts, energy thresholds, creature size/age, recently damaged, carrying food, etc.)
• Save & load filter presets
• Visual net mesh + edge feedback while scooping
• Optional net upgrades (capacity, strength, range)
v1.8
Aerosol Sprays – Polish & Depth
• Better visual targeting and spray effects
• Limited uses / cooldown system (or energy cost)
• More distinct spray types and stronger differentiation between effects
• Improved feedback when spraying (particles, sound, creature reactions)
v1.9+
Music Reactivity System
• Upload & select songs from a dedicated folder
• Real-time audio analysis (frequency bands, beat detection, energy)
• Multiple reactive parameters controlled by different parts of the music:
– Vortex strength & fall speed
– Food node size pulsing (green/blue)
– Red hazard / cursor hazard spawn frequency
– Energy wall pulsing and movement
– Creature movement jitter / responsiveness
– Screen shake or particle intensity on bass
• Sliders to map frequency bands to different game systems
• Presets for different music genres / reactivity styles
• Toggleable music player window with visualizer
Detailed implementation plan to be provided by user
v2.0+
Treasure Room & Inventory Sink
• "BS Treasure Store" with 10+ overpriced, silly, high-cost items
• 10-slot player inventory that must be managed before buying more
• Dedicated Treasure Room where items can be tossed or carefully arranged
• Basic physics for items piling up in the corner
• Future potential: use accumulated treasure as resources or decorative flex
