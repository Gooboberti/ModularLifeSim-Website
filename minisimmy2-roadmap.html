Future Development Phases

v1.7 Series – Major Redesign Active
Energy Walls as Configurable Barriers & Selective Filters
v1.71 – v1.74 (Foundation)
• Vortex-immune walls by default + temporary vulnerability after damage
• Drag-to-sweep placement with live preview
• Basic per-wall filter modes + number key pre-selection
• Walls no longer do damage by default (damage becomes opt-in)
v1.75 (Current – Menu System)
• Clicking Energy Wall button opens a placement menu
• Menu includes different wall types: Solid Barrier, Filter/Pusher Wall, etc.
• Filter walls open a sub-menu with detailed options:
  – Select which creatures to affect (Predators, Harvesters, White/Red particles, Top 10% modules, etc.)
  – Choose behavior: Push back / Repel / Capture / Segregate
• Unaffected creatures pass through normally
• Damage, breaking, and red-particle interaction become optional toggles
v1.76
• Polish the menu UI (nice icons, categories, search/filter in sub-menu)
• Multiple filter behaviors working simultaneously
• Visual feedback on walls showing what they affect (colored icons / glows)
• Ability to edit filters on existing walls via right-click or inspector
v1.77 – v1.78
• Advanced behaviors: Capture (pull creatures along the wall), Segregate (sort creatures to one side)
• Opt-in modules: Damage over time, Break-on-impact, Red particle absorption/empowerment
• Wall templates / presets you can save and quickly place
• Energy cost or maintenance for complex filter walls
v1.79+
• "Smart" walls that learn or react to creature behavior over time
• Combining multiple filter rules with priority/AND/OR logic
• Wall groups / linked walls that share filter settings
• Full integration with the future Aerosol and other systems
v1.8+
Aerosol Sprays + Later Systems
Aerosol system, Music Reactivity, and Treasure Room remain on the long-term roadmap but are paused while we fully develop the Energy Wall + Filter system.
This roadmap is the living source of truth for development direction.
