The Paragon / Prestige System

Transform MiniSimmy2 from a single-run sandbox into a legacy-driven experience with meaningful meta-progression, persistent lineage, and player-driven evolution across multiple "universes".

Core Paragon Mechanics
The prestige loop foundation
01
Prestige / Map Reset
Full world reset triggered by player at high cost. New run begins with inherited advantages.
02
100M High Roller Points Cost
Significant but achievable late-game threshold. Encourages long, optimized runs and strategic play.
03
Aetherium Crystals (Meta Currency)
Earned on prestige. Spent on permanent meta-upgrades between runs. Primary prestige reward.
Lineage & Neural Memory
Persistent creature evolution
04
Player-Chosen Lineage Genes
At prestige, player selects 1–3 genes/traits from a pool or from their best creatures. These influence starting population in next run.
05
Strong Neural / Memory Preservation
Carry over behavioral biases, learned preferences, and neural connection strengths (or simplified version). Creatures start "smarter" or with inherited tendencies.
06
Meta Upgrades Shop
Spend Aetherium on permanent improvements: higher starting energy, better module unlock rates, increased Paragon spawn chance, stronger social pressure baseline, etc.
Paragon Creatures & Spawn Frequency
Elite legacy beings that shape future runs
HIGH PRIORITY
Special Visuals
Golden aura, unique module glows, larger size, particle trails, or distinct silhouette. Clearly distinguishable from normal creatures.
Carry-over Bonuses
Start with higher tier modules, innate resistance, faster learning, or unique abilities (e.g. stronger pheromone, better vortex resistance).
Spawn Frequency Upgrades
Meta upgrades increase chance of Paragon creatures appearing naturally or via special events. Can become a core progression vector.
