MiniSimmy2 Roadmap

Evolutionary modular swarm simulation with day/night resources, vortex dynamics, pheromone signaling, energy recycling, and emergent intelligence.

CURRENT VERSION: v0.21
  • ✅ Day/Night cycle with dynamic food resets + zone-specific module bonuses
  • ✅ Smooth gradual vortex direction transitions (cosine-based)
  • ✅ Pheromone trails with day/night-aware following + firefly attraction
  • ✅ Food Blooms + Energy Orbs on death (energy recycling)
  • ✅ Module efficiency system (Harvester & Resistant reduce drain)
  • ✅ Clickable creature inspector with animated 6-4-6 neural net
  • ✅ Lineage tracking, children list, kill count, reproduction cooldown
  • ✅ Wide live parameter sliders + master volume control
PHASE 1 • IN PROGRESS

Module Expansion + Stronger Neural Influence

Add new meaningful module types and make the neural network have stronger, more visible impact on creature behavior.

New Modules

  • Explorer — Significantly better vortex escape + increased random exploration when far from goals.
  • Communicator — Deposits stronger and longer-lasting pheromones. Nearby creatures get slight signal boost.
  • Defender (future) — Reduces energy loss when clustered with others.

Neural Net Agency

Amplify the effect of key neural inputs (pheromone density, neighbor fitness, vortex pull) so creatures visibly adapt their movement and signaling strategies during their lifetime.

PHASE 2

Deeper Evolution & Genetic Memory

  • Actual neural weight inheritance + mutation across generations
  • Lineage-specific adaptations (some bloodlines become better at certain strategies)
  • Visual indicators in inspector for inherited neural "personalities"
  • Long-term evolution statistics graph (average modules, energy, signaling strength over time)
PHASE 3

Social Structures & Emergent Groups

  • Basic flocking / alignment behavior when near similar modules
  • Temporary "clans" or loose groups that share pheromone strength
  • Cultural transmission: successful movement/signaling patterns weakly inherited via observation + pheromones
PHASE 4

Environmental Richness & Communication

  • Multiple dynamic smaller food patches + temporary environmental events
  • Enhanced audio: module-specific sounds + success/failure signals
  • More complex predator evolution and role specialization
  • Visual polish: movement trails, energy auras, better creature rendering
Roadmap is actively updated. Goal: Create meaningful emergent swarm intelligence through modular bodies, neural adaptation, signaling, and energy conservation.
