MiniSimmy2 Roadmap

Evolutionary swarm simulation with modular bodies, day/night resources, vortex dynamics, and emergent signaling.

PHASE 1 • NEXT

Ant + Bee + Firefly Hybrid Signaling System

Implement a hybrid swarm intelligence layer that combines:

  • Ant Colony Optimization (ACO) — Virtual pheromone trails that creatures deposit when successful (eating, reproducing, high energy). Trails decay over time.
  • Firefly Algorithm — Creatures are attracted to “brighter” neighbors (higher fitness = energy + offspring + signaling quality).
  • Artificial Bee Colony (ABC) role differentiation via modules: Harvesters act as exploiters, Sensors as signal amplifiers/scouts.

Neural Net Enhancement

Add 2 new inputs to the existing 6-4-6 neural net:

  • Local pheromone density
  • Average brightness/fitness of nearby creatures

This allows creatures to learn signaling-aware behaviors during their lifetime.

Genetic / Inheritance Benefits

Modules and neural connections that improve pheromone sensitivity or effective signaling have higher chance of being passed to offspring when the parent was successful. This creates true multi-generational learning through both cultural (pheromone) and genetic channels.

PHASE 2
Advanced Emergent Communication
Chorus formation, rhythmic signaling protocols, simple “grammar” in sound patterns, regional dialects based on successful local strategies.
PHASE 3
Role Specialization & Division of Labor
Stronger module-driven behavioral specialization. Harvesters focus on resource zones, Sensors become mobile signal relays, Movers act as scouts.
PHASE 4
Cultural Transmission
Successful signaling strategies and movement patterns can be learned and passed culturally (via pheromone strength + observation) in addition to genetic inheritance.
This roadmap is actively updated as the simulation evolves. The goal is meaningful emergent swarm intelligence that improves both individual lifetime performance and long-term evolutionary success.
