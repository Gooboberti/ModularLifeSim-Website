Vision & Emotional Core

This is not just another virtual pet. It is the foundational minigame layer of a larger bidirectional ecosystem between two simulations:

  • Here (SYNTHO-LAB): Direct, intimate, emotional raising of individual creatures in a tech-bio lab/ranch setting. Tamagotchi-style real-time needs, rich interactions, visual personality, and gradual gene refinement through lived experience.
  • MiniSimmy (connected repo): The larger modular p5.js ecosystem simulation — swarms, evolution, social dynamics, economy, predators, environmental cycles. Brains/genes are produced, tested, and iterated there at scale.

The magic is the closed loop:

1. Import a brain/gene package from MiniSimmy as an egg → drops into this lab.
2. Choose or generate a body/avatar (different forms have innate temperaments, movement styles, sensory preferences, metabolic rates).
3. Raise it with love, discipline, play, and care. The body phenotype expresses and refines the brain through thousands of micro-interactions and outcome feedback.
4. At key thresholds (evolution, high bond, specific achievements), export a refined egg containing updated genes + neural/behavioral data back to MiniSimmy or other bodies.

Different bodies "train" brains differently. A sturdy Guardian body might stabilize volatile high-creativity brains. A nimble Scout body amplifies exploration and risk-taking traits. The body is not just a vessel — it is an active shaper of the mind it carries.

Creature Types, Temperaments & Base Personalities

Each base body has innate preferences, decay rates, visual language, and future movement/behavior styles. These exist independently of any imported brain. The brain modulates and is modulated by them.

Aetherfloof
Playful • Affectionate • Social
Visual: Round fluffy body, big expressive eyes, ear tufts, stubby tail. Gentle bobbing idle.
Preferences: Sweet foods, petting/cuddling, group play (future).
Decay bias: Higher happiness decay if lonely. Slower hunger.
Brain interaction: Amplifies social/cooperative traits in imported brains. Stabilizes high-anxiety brains.
Drakeling
Curious • Independent • Bold
Visual: Sleeker elongated form, wing stubs or fins, sharper features, tail. More dynamic idle animation.
Preferences: Protein-rich foods, active play/chase, exploration toys.
Decay bias: Faster energy drain during play. Enjoys variety.
Brain interaction: Boosts novelty-seeking and risk/reward traits. Good for high-creativity or explorer brains. Can make cautious brains bolder.
Lumibug
Calm • Observant • Energy-based
Visual: Orb or segmented glowing form, soft bioluminescent pulses synced to mood/energy. Gentle hover/bob.
Preferences: Light/energy items, quiet observation, cleaning/grooming rituals.
Decay bias: Very slow hunger. Happiness tied to light/hygiene. Sensitive to "dark" periods (future day/night).
Brain interaction: Excellent for meditative or pattern-recognition brains. Slows down hyperactive brains. Enhances sensory processing traits.
Prototype currently cycles between these three. Full version will have many more + hybrid/mutant forms unlocked via genes + evolution.

MiniSimmy Integration & Bidirectional Brain/Gene Refinement

THE LOOP
Step 1 — Import
MiniSimmy produces a brain/gene package (simple trait vector or serialized behavior params). Exported as special "Egg" item. Drag or hatch into SYNTHO-LAB.
Step 2 — Embody
Choose body type (or procedural from genome). Body provides base temperament, metabolism, sensory biases, movement style. Brain is "installed" and begins modulating behavior.
Step 3 — Refine
Thousands of interactions + care outcomes provide training signal. Positive care in aligned body strengthens desired traits. Misalignment or neglect creates interesting "stress adaptations".
Step 4 — Export
At evolution, high bond milestones, or manual "Refine & Export", the lab packages updated genome + experience-modified brain data into a new egg. Ready for MiniSimmy or another body.
Body as Phenotype Trainer
The avatar is not passive. It actively shapes the brain it hosts through its built-in preferences and interaction physics. This is the "body refines brain" direction.
Brain as Behavioral Overlay
Imported brain adds nuance: occasional "decisions", unique idle animations or movement quirks, preference shifts, even new interaction outcomes. Brain refines body expression.
Example mapping (future implementation):
MiniSimmy brain.curiosity = 0.9 → Drakeling body amplifies exploration actions + higher play drive. Floof body might dampen it slightly toward social curiosity instead.
