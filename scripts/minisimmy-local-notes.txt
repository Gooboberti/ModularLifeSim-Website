local-002-center-pan-fix (CONFIRMED WORKING)
--------------------------------------------
Symptom: Press 3 to center on creature — food/vortex slide with the view; press 3 again and food "resets."

Cause: syncVortexWorldCenter() in center-follow mode called syncFoodZonesFromScreen(), rebinding food/vortex world coords to fixed screen positions every frame while tickMapCameraFollow() panned the viewport.

Fix:
- syncVortexWorldCenter() always uses fixed world anchors: mapWorldVortexX/Y, mapWorldGreenX/Y, mapWorldBlueX/Y
- Do NOT call syncFoodZonesFromScreen() during center follow
- Always draw world-anchored backdrops (drawVortexBackdropWorld, drawFoodZoneBackdropWorld)
- tickMapCameraFollow() only pans via Viewport.centerOnWorld() when mapCameraMode === 'center'

Do not re-add screen-locked food sync during center follow.

local-003-probe-toggle
----------------------
Press 2 toggles scoop probe (requires scoop deployed via 0). Replaces offspring jump on key 2.
Flag: creature._scoopProbeDeployed (default true on scoop deploy). isScoopProbeDeployed() gates probe geometry and prune/tug logic.

local-004-tendril-toggle
------------------------
Press 5 toggles scoop tendrils (requires scoop deployed via 0). Replaces select-alpha on key 5 (Alpha button still in inspector).
Flag: creature._scoopTendrilsDeployed (default true on scoop deploy). isScoopTendrilsDeployed() gates tentacle geometry, tail ejection, tentacle contacts, and tentacle particle pulls. Bubble + probe still work when tendrils off.

local-007-squid-lattice-bonuses
-------------------------------
Colony creatures tethered when a jellyfish squid steals their resonance lace (stealLatticeForScoop → _scoopStolenLinks[].otherRef) gain bonuses while that scoop+lattice is active:
- getSquidLatticeBonuses(creature) — scales with link count, squid tier, and lace memory
- Higher reproduction chance (reproMult ~1.3–1.7+)
- Higher honey deposit chance (honeyMult ~1.45–2.0+)
- Higher energy efficiency (energyGainMult up, drainMult down)
Tracks _squidLatticeBonusPulse on linked creatures for future visuals.

local-005-squid-belly-pop
-------------------------
Jellyfish/squid belly inventory + tier pop brood (ported from 0e65f25, extended to tiers 0-3).
- `_jellyfishTier` 0-3 (default 2); legacy `_jellyfishSize` 0→T1, 1→T2 when tier unset.

- Auto pop rolls legendary silver/gold and vaults when possible. Probe/tendril toggles from local-004 preserved.
