THE VISION

Beyond individual agents.
Toward genuine multi-scale intelligence.

Most artificial life projects focus on evolving better individual creatures. This project takes a fundamentally different approach: it explores what happens when complexity is allowed to stack across multiple layers of organization.

The goal is not just more sophisticated agents, but the emergence of higher-order entities — structures that contain other complex structures, with their own identity, goals, and internal dynamics.

This is the path toward Thronglet-level emergence: recursive, multi-scale beings capable of genuine collective agency, culture, and open-ended evolution.

HOW IT WORKS

Core Systems of MiniSimmy2

Creatures & Modules

Every creature carries a combination of modules that shape its behavior, strengths, and role in the ecosystem. Modules can be upgraded through merging into higher tiers.

Harvester
Sensor
Mover
Resistant
Explorer
Communicator

Modules create strong selection pressure for physical form, behavior, and specialization.

Neural Brain System

Every creature has a small neural network that makes real-time decisions. The brain is copied to offspring and mutated during reproduction. This creates heritable "personalities" and decision-making styles across generations.

Brains influence food drive, hazard avoidance, vortex escape, social behavior, exploration, reproduction will, and predator tendency.

Lifespan & Aging

Creatures now have dynamic lifespans. Higher-generation creatures live longer by default. Resistant modules add bonus lifespan, creating meaningful trade-offs between survival and mobility.

Aging visibly affects movement speed and appearance. Death from old age is now a natural part of the simulation.

Gene Vault & Bloodlines

Extract high-performing creatures into eggs and store them permanently across Prestige resets. The Vault preserves both modules and neural brains, allowing you to cultivate powerful lineages over many runs.

This is the core long-term progression system of the project.
EMERGENT BEHAVIOR

What Actually Emerges

Role Specialization

Creatures naturally differentiate into Foragers, Survivors, Explorers, and Social Coordinators through module interaction and selection pressure — not hardcoded roles.

Social Grouping & Pressure

Communicator modules create pheromone clusters. Large groups can trigger predator conversion pressure, favoring certain bloodlines.

Vortex Mastery

Strong Mover and Explorer modules enable escape from the central vortex or strategic edge positioning, creating spatial niches.

Persistent Bloodlines

Through the Gene Vault, successful genomes and brains persist across Prestige resets, enabling deliberate long-term lineage development.
