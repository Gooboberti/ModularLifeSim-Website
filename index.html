THE VISION

Beyond individual agents.
Toward genuine multi-scale intelligence.

Most artificial life projects focus on evolving better individual creatures. This project takes a fundamentally different approach: it explores what happens when complexity is allowed to stack across multiple layers of organization.

The goal is not just more sophisticated agents, but the emergence of higher-order entities — structures that contain other complex structures, with their own identity, goals, and internal dynamics.

This is the path toward what we’re calling Thronglet-level emergence: recursive, multi-scale beings capable of genuine collective agency, culture, and open-ended evolution.

HOW IT WORKS

Core Mechanics of MiniSimmy

Creatures & Modules

Every creature has a set of modules that define its behavior, strengths, and role. Modules can be stacked and upgraded through merging into higher tiers for significantly stronger effects. Evolution acts on which modules are present and how they combine.

Harvester — Strong food zone attraction, excellent for energy & growth.
Sensor — Detects & avoids predators. Key survival advantage.
Mover — Better vortex escape & mobility. Resists central pull.
Resistant — Reduces energy drain. Strong for long-term survival.
Explorer — Encourages spreading out. Boosts genetic diversity.
Communicator — Releases pheromones. Drives social grouping & coordination.

Modules create strong selection pressure for physical form, behavior, and specialization.

Vortex & Environment

A powerful spiraling vortex constantly pulls creatures toward the center. Creatures with strong Mover or Explorer modules can resist or escape it more effectively, creating interesting spatial dynamics and escape strategies.

Day/Night Cycle: Food zones shift (green during day, blue at night). Harvester modules greatly increase attraction to these vital resource patches. Environmental cycles (Fertile / Normal / Drought) add further strategic depth.

Gene Vault & Bloodlines

The Gene Vault is the core long-term progression system. Extract strong creatures into eggs and store them permanently across Prestige resets. The first 2 slots are unlocked by default; more can be purchased with Aetherium Crystals.

Creatures stored in the Gene Vault influence future generations when Prestige babies are spawned. This enables deliberate bloodline development and preservation of successful genomes.

Prestige, Furnace & Economy

High Roller Points (HRP): Primary score earned from simulation performance, population management, and longevity.

Prestige: Resets the current simulation but preserves your Gene Vault. Converts HRP into Aetherium Crystals and grants a starting bonus for the next run. Stronger vault = stronger future runs.

Furnace: Burn Aetherium Crystals to activate a powerful x100 score multiplier for 5 minutes (extendable). The ultimate high-score engine for dedicated runs.

SIMULATION SYSTEMS

The Living Loop

Every frame the simulation updates:

  • Day/Night Cycle — Shifts food zone colors and availability (green day, blue night), affecting Harvester strategies.
  • Vortex & Pheromones — Updates spiraling force, particle effects, and chemical signals that influence movement and grouping.
  • Creatures — Movement, energy metabolism, module effects, reproduction, death, and behavioral responses.
  • Reproduction & Death — New creatures inherit and mutate module combinations; weak or unlucky ones are removed.
  • UI & Progression — Real-time population, score (HRP), time, Gene Vault status, and Prestige/Furnace controls.

The loop supports long-running sessions with emergent population dynamics, role specialization, and strategic depth through module synergies and environmental pressures.

EMERGENT BEHAVIOR

What Actually Emerges

Role Specialization

Creatures naturally differentiate into Foragers (Harvester-heavy), Survivors (Sensor + Resistant), Explorers (Explorer + Mover), and Social Coordinators (Communicator) through the interaction of modules, environmental pressures, and reproduction. Roles are not hardcoded — they emerge from selection.

Social Grouping & Pheromone Dynamics

Communicator modules release pheromones that attract others, creating clusters, choruses of movement, and coordinated responses. Higher densities can trigger predator emergence, favoring Sensor-equipped bloodlines.

Vortex Mastery & Spatial Strategy

Strong Mover and Explorer modules allow escape from the central vortex or strategic positioning at fertile edges. This creates spatial niches and selection for mobility vs. central resource focus.

Persistent Bloodlines via Gene Vault

Successful genomes can be extracted and preserved. Over multiple Prestige cycles, players develop stable, high-performing bloodlines that carry advantages into new runs — early persistent identity across resets.

Economic & Multiplier Strategies

The Furnace system rewards long, optimized runs with massive score multipliers. Combined with Gene Vault planning, it creates deep meta-strategy around when to Prestige and how to build for high-score pushes.
