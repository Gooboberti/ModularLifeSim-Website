MiniSimmy2 — Legend & Guide

Environmental Cycles
The world cycles through Fertile → Normal → Drought periods. Fertile gives higher energy gain and reproduction. Drought increases drain and makes survival harder.
Modules (Tiers I–V)
Harvester
Increases energy from food zones and white particles. Higher tiers = larger pickup range.
Sensor
Better hazard detection and avoidance. Higher tiers = predictive dodging.
Mover
Helps escape the vortex center. Higher tiers = better spiral escape.
Resistant
Reduces energy drain, especially useful in Drought phases.
Explorer
Increases exploration and random movement. Helps avoid getting stuck.
Communicator
Strengthens pheromone signals and hazard warnings.
3 of the same module = upgrade to next tier.
Yellow → Brown Honey Blooms
Creatures deposit honey that slowly moves toward food nodes. When it stops, it becomes a new food patch.
Green Energy Orbs
Released on death. Pulled by vortex to food nodes and restore food (gives High Roller Points).
Red Hazard Particles
Dangerous threats (AOE + DoT). Can be toggled in the Cheats Store.
Cursor Hazard Tool
Mouse becomes a damaging red particle. Buy in the Cheats Store.
Current Version: v0.96
This version introduces the Energy Beam Hazard Chain system, improved wall splitting with immunity, stronger purple DoT, and red particle absorption mechanics. Future updates will continue to be versioned here.
Hazard Cursor Absorption
When the Cursor Hazard is active, it absorbs nearby Red Particles on contact. A satisfying absorb sound plays. This is a great way to clear hazards while doing damage.
Energy Wall Empowerment
Red Particles that hit an Energy Wall are absorbed. Each hit:
• Causes the wall to shake visually
• Slowly shifts its color toward red
• Increments the absorption counter

After exactly 5 hits, the wall becomes fully empowered:
• Moves much slower in the vortex (25% speed)
• On contact with creatures: instantly turns them into Predators
• Still bounces and applies purple damage, but now has this powerful conversion effect.
Detailed Mechanics: Brain & Sensing
Every creature has a "brain" simulated through its modules and simple rules:

Sensor Module: Increases detection range and quality for hazards, walls, and bullets. Higher tiers allow predictive dodging from farther away.

Avoidance Logic: Creatures constantly scan for nearby dangers (Red Hazards, Energy Walls, spaceship bullets, persistent hazard fields). They calculate an avoidance vector and steer away. The strength of avoidance scales with how close and how many sensors they have.

Thought System: Creatures display short thoughts ("Dodging hazard!", "Burning!", "Absorbed by empowered wall", etc.) based on what they're reacting to. This gives insight into their "mental state".

Learning / Evolution: Creatures that survive longer (especially by avoiding hazards well) reproduce more successfully. Over time, populations evolve better sensor counts and avoidance behavior. The spaceship and empowered walls act as strong selective pressures.

Module Synergy: Harvesters gather more efficiently near food. Sensors help avoid death. Movers escape the vortex better. The combination of modules + environment + hazards creates emergent survival strategies.
