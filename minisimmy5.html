MiniSimmy2 — Legend & Guide

Environmental Cycles
The world cycles through Fertile → Normal → Drought periods. Fertile gives higher energy gain and reproduction. Drought increases drain and makes survival harder.
Modules (Tiers I–X)
Harvester
Increases energy from food zones and white particles. Higher tiers = larger pickup range.
Sensor
Better hazard detection and avoidance. Higher tiers = predictive dodging.
Mover
Helps escape the vortex center. Higher tiers = better spiral escape.
Resistant
Reduces energy drain, especially useful in Drought phases.
Explorer
Increases exploration and random movement. Helps avoid getting stuck.
Communicator
Strengthens pheromone signals and hazard warnings.

Voidheart Prestige (Incubator)
Forge Voidheart Shards in Inventory (1 Billion Crystals each). In the Incubator, spend shards to prestige a creature: the brain is preserved, all ordinary modules are stripped, and a permanent core specialization is distilled from the exact module composition (hybrids like Void Garden, Storm Shell, Null Harmony, etc.). Cost scales with generation and prior prestiges. Use Cycle Again when a cycle completes to keep incubating without claiming.
Aether Tech Tree
Open Tech Tree below the main menu. Spend crystals within each tier, then pay the gate cost to unlock deeper tiers. Perks include Crystal Resonance (bonus crystals on prestige), Incubator Insight (faster cycles), Module Resonance, Anomaly Spawner, Evolution Leak, Crystal Lattice (vault score + multi-echo spawns), Auto-Prestige Protocol, and God-Tier Ascendant modules. Aether Sovereigns (crown icon) are a premium currency shown in the top bar — spending coming soon.
