MiniSimmy2 — Legend & System Info

Core Colors & Specialization
● Green — Harvester dominant (better gathering)
● Blue — Sensor dominant (hazard detection)
● Purple — Mover dominant (faster movement)
● Pink — Resistant dominant (lower drain)
● Yellow — Explorer dominant (exploration)
● Violet — Communicator dominant (group bonuses)
Module Tiers
Tier 1 — Basic module. Standard bonus.
Tier 2 — Formed by fusing 3× Tier 1 of the same type. Stronger bonus + yellow border.
Tier 3 — Formed by fusing 3× Tier 2. Very powerful.
Tier 4 — Formed by fusing 3× Tier 3. Elite modules.
Red Hazard Particles
Dangerous red particles. High Sensor count helps detect and avoid them. Impact causes energy drain + panic behavior toward food zones.
Vortex & Day/Night
Vortex pulls toward center and changes direction at dawn/dusk. Green zone is active during day, Blue at night.
