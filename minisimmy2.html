Legend & Guide

Energy Walls — Click the shield icon then drag on the map to draw glowing barriers. Creatures bounce gently and take purple damage over time.
Floating Numbers — Very rare critical hits only. Nearby crits are automatically grouped and summed into one clean number (Diablo-style). Toggle with the # icon.
Modules & Tiers — Creatures collect colored modules. 3 of the same type fuse into the next tier (up to Tier V). Higher tiers = stronger bonuses.
Social Pressure — In large groups, minority/outcast creatures can turn into predators.
Vortex — Centered spiral that pulls everything inward. Strongest at noon and midnight.
