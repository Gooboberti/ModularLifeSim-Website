Educational Guide — 6 Inputs (Matching Main Simulation)

1. Food Angle
Direction to nearest food relative to facing.
2. Food Distance
Proximity to nearest food (0–1).
3. Danger / Predator Distance
How close the threat is.
4. Energy Level
Current stamina (affects behavior).
5. Memory (Recurrent)
Internal state carried across time.
6. Bias / Internal Drive
Heritable trait (aggression vs caution).
